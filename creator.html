Chhimi Dorji

Student, The Royal Academy (Paro Pangbisa)

Chhimi, hailing from **Thimphu**, is currently studying at the **Royal Academy Paro Pangbisa**. Known for a deep curiosity and a wide variety of **hobbies**, Chhimi embodies a passion for both traditional Bhutanese arts and modern expressive forms.

A true multidisciplinary learner, Chhimi's interests span across different fields, reflecting a constant desire to learn, analyze, and apply creative thinking to every challenge.
